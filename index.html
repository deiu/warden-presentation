Engineering Culture
Before
  • No technical direction and focus
  • No process (chaos)
  • Impossible to ship on time
  • Poor quality: lots of bugs in production
What I did
  • Gradually introduced process
  • Defined clear ownership and responsibilities
  • Product-tech-commercial alignment and visibility
  • Regular 1-1s, mentorship, active mediation on team tensions
After 2-3 months
  • Consistently hitting weekly releases
  • Release quality improved
  • Faster to adapt to last minute commercial asks
